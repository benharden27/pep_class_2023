Your research team will prepare for answering research questions by delivering a brief introductory presentation about your topic area in the region of interest (Rhode Island Sound). Everyone should contribute to the presentation and each team member should speak for 2-3 mins. In your presentation, you could cover:

  • Key scientific and regional background information
  • What is known and not known about your topic
  • What question(s) you will be seeking to answer
